Koichi

Koichi was a high school student in the small Japanese town of Morioh Town, he was the first to encounter Jotaro Kujo and Josuke Higashikata and the first to worry about the dangers that inhabit the town. Koichi investigated the city in which he had lived for years, one day while walking with Josuke he was captured by Keicho Nijimura and Okuyasu Nijimura , at that moment Koichi was pierced by the Stand-Creating Arrow. After Josuke and Keicho's fight, Koichi awakens his Stand, Echoes Act 1 . Koichi is kidnapped by one of his classmates who is in love with him, she keeps him captive in a presidential suite secluded from the city. Koichi manages to escape from her after defeating her in a fight and saving her life as well. He later ends up falling in love with her, as she becomes good. Koichi also confronts Rohan Kishibe , who hypnotizes him with his Stand to make him come to his house every day so he can steal his experiences to write his manga . Later they become good friends. Koichi accompanies Jotaro to look for clues about Yoshikage Kira , but they did not know that they would end up finding him and having a fight against his Sheer Heart Attack and against Kira himself, who almost killed Koichi if not for the intervention of Jotaro and Josuke. After defeating Kira, Koichi returns to a normal life in Morioh, even making his relationship with Yukako official. Two years later he is sent to Naples , Italy by Jotaro in search of a man named Haruno Shiobana, who is none other than Giorno Giovanna, he later discovers that he is a Stand user and son of the antagonist DIO.
