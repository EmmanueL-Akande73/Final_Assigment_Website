Gilgamesh (ギルガメッシュ? , Girugamesshu ) , also called King of Heroes (英雄王? , Eiyū-Ō ) or Archer (アーチャー? , Āchā ) due to his class, is a fictional character and one of the main antagonists of the English visual novel Fate /stay night , written by Kinoko Nasu and illustrated by Takashi Takeuchi. Gilgamesh is a very arogant person and belives every good thing in this world is hes, but he is also a good and great king.
